Where is the nearest bank?

There is a Natwest along Main Street, a bit further up from the Charles.

Main Street, Heslington, York, YO1 5YL

Where is the nearest cash point?

Cash Machines can be found at the following locations on or close to campus:

Market Square, Central Campus

Vanbrugh Walkway, Central Campus

The Student Centre, Goodricke College, Central Campus

Barclays Bank, Heslington Road, Heslington.

HSBC, Heslington Road, Heslington

Lloyds TSB, Heslington Road Heslington.

Where is the nearest hairdresser?

Ken Fairburn, or "Campus Ken" is an on-campus hairdresser and has cut York students hair for 23 years. He cuts hair Tuesday to Thursday.

Go to Langwith porters and ask for his room number

Where is the nearest pharmacy?

As there's no chemist's on campus, picking up prescriptions can be a chore.

The closest are:

Whitworth Chemists 275 Melrosegate (from campus, walk along University Road and Green Dykes Lane to the Shell garage- it's just through the traffic lights on the right). Open Mon-Fri 9am-6pm, and Sat 9am-4pm.

Moss Chemists 153a Tang Hall Lane. Open Mon-Fri 9am-1pm and 2-6pm, Sat just 9am-1pm.

Badger Hill Pharmacy 35 Yarbrugh Way Hull Road Open Mon-Fri 9am-5:30pm Sat 9am-2pm

Hepworth MS Chemists 101 Main Street Fulford Open Mon-Fri 9am-1pm and 2-5:30pm Sat 9am-1pm

Superdrug 15-17 Market St York

Boots 5 St. Marys Sq York

Boots 48 Coney Street York

Where is the nearest post office?

Go out of Derwent onto University Road and turn right, head towards the Charles. It's a bit further down.

Main Street, Heslington, YO10 5EA

Where is the nearest sandwich shop?

Browns, is a fantastic little delicatessen which does a wide variety of sandwiches. It is further up from the Charles, on the left hand side of the road.

5-16 Main Street, Heslington, North Yorkshire YO10 5EA
