Chiifen Hiu

Chiifen joined the Yorker in October 2009 to write for the news section. She became News Features Coordinator later that year and Deputy Politics Editor in January 2010. She became Politics Editor in April. In chilly October 2010, after returning from a proper summer in Malaysia, she was elected Editor of the Yorker. Chiifen is a second year Psychology student, and contrary to popular belief is not crazy, except when it comes to the Yorker of course. She loves to volunteer and sings to old people every other week. Apart from that her time is spent on her very serious TV series addiction.

editor@theyorker.co.uk

Richard Simpson

Rich is Technical Director of The Yorker, and has been involved from the beginning his second year. Rich is not only a technical guru for the Journalists but is also responsible for ensuring the development and success of the technical side of the website. He is a second year Computer Scientist. Outside of The Yorker Rich enjoys refereeing, performing magic, playing the piano, cricket and football and going for a pint with a few mates down to the pub. Rich also writes an occasional article for The Yorker - when he isn't too busy.

webmaster@theyorker.co.uk

Nick Evans

Nick is a Computer Science student who co-founded The Yorker in 2006 with Daniel Ashby. He spent a year on an industrial placement with Detica during 2007/08, when his duties as Technical Director were passed Richard Ingle. On his return in 2008/09, Nick was appointed Non-operational Director. Nick is known for being a drama-enthusiast, took part in Fusion in 2009, and featured in Happily Ever After Soc's production of High School Musical as Coach Bolton. He is also enjoys skiing and snowboarding, particularly on YUSnow’s easter trip to Val Thorens.

nick_evans@theyorker.co.uk

Richard Mitchell

Richard Mitchell is a non-operational director of The Yorker and helps guide the company's long-term strategy in the achievement of its aims and objectives. Having been a student at York since 2004 as both an undergraduate and postgraduate, his experience both in industry at Airbus UK and of the university and its unions are invaluable to The Yorker. He is very proud of the strong tradition of student media at York and believes that it is key to furthering democracy and accountability in the unions. Mitch is into politics, cycling, music and power kites as well as a casual interest in damn near everything. York is his Rushmore.

richard_mitchell@theyorker.co.uk

Marie Thouaille

Marie is The Yorker's most recently appointed non-operational director, having taken on the role in early 2010. She was Editor of the site from January 2009 to January 2010. She started out as Film and TV Editor in October 2007. In February 2008 Marie became Blog Editor and in October 2008 she was elected Deputy Editor. She lives in Tahiti, where the average temperatures are 24 to 30C all year. We are all extremely jealous.

marie_thouaille@theyorker.co.uk
