Cocktail Society: Cocktail Roses Countdown


Celebrate the Roses in style
Laura Reynolds
So we here at Cocktail Society are getting pretty excited about Roses and with only a week to go we thought we’d start giving you guys some ideas for showing you’re York, or Lancaster, pride in your drinks.

caption here

Day 1

For white Yorkists:

Velvet Hammer

  • 1 measure triple sec
  • 1 measure crème de cacao (preferably white)
  • 4 measures cream

Place all the ingredients into a glass with ice, shake and strain into a cocktail glass.

For red Lancastrians:

Strawberry Daiquiri

  • 1 measure white rum
  • 1 measure strawberry liqueur
  • A dash of lemon juice
  • 4 ripe strawberries

Simply place the ingredients along with a handful of crushed ice into a blender and zap until well-blended. Strain into a cocktail glass and garnish with a strawberry.

Day 2

White Lady Cocktail

  • 1½ ounces gin
  • ¾ Cointreau
  • ¾ ounce freshly squeezed lemon juice

[Some White Lady recipes include an egg white for volume and texture – add one if you want a bit of frothy oomph to your cocktail]

Combine all the ingredients in a cocktail shaker with ice. Shake well and strain into a cocktail glass. Simple, sophisticated, and strong!

Red Berry Mojito

  • Handful mint leaves
  • 1 shot sugar syrup
  • 4 strawberries
  • 4 raspberries
  • Juice of a lime
  • 1½ shots white rum
  • Soda water

Put the mint, sugar syrup, berries and lime juice in a highball glass. Using a wooden spoon, lightly mash together, trying to avoid breaking the leaves. Fill the glass with crushed ice and add the rum. Stir well. Top up with soda water, and serve.
