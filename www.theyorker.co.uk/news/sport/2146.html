BUCS preview


BUCS action gets underway tomorrow with rugby and football taking centre stage on 22 Acres
Tuesday, 21st October 2008
Michael Sneddon
BUCS kicks off its 2008/09 season in York with a bumper day of fixtures for York's home supporters.

The pick of the 22 Acres action sees York's first team football and rugby play against Leeds Met. seconds and Northumbria thirds respectively. The highlight of the indoor matches sees York table tennis firsts play Sheffield firsts and hopefully The Yorker's 'ones to watch' won't disappoint.

The first competitive BUCS action on the new astroturf kicks off at 12:00 with the stand out matches seeing the men's hockey firsts play Northumbria with the women's hockey firsts playing Leeds' seconds just before.
