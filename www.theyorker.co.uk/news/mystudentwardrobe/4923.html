MSW - Amy Clark


Tuesday, 1st June 2010
Cem Turhan
Amy Clark is a first year History student from London in Langwith College.

The blazer was from Zara which I brought at Easter for £65. I like the lining and it’s kind of a little bit 80's. I wore it to Salvation though and now it’s filthy.

The necklace was £1 from primark. I like the length.

The dress was £15 in the sale from Miss Selfridge. I like the bold prints on a dark background which is a general theme in my wardrobe. I also really like the flower buttons.

The brogues were £60/70 from Massimo Dutti. They're made out of really soft leather and are very comfy. I spent so much money on them because I think they’re classic and I can wear them forever, even when I’m 70.

I got the hairband at Bestival last summer when I was fairly inebriated and spent far too much money on something that is made out of paper flowers. I always put stuff in my hair because it’s so boring by itself.

Click here to view whole outfit

Style tip: Mix expensive classics with sale items and cheap Primark stuff that will only last for one season to keep up to date without using up all your student loan.

Style icon: Kate Moss; I admire her style but I don’t copy it.
