MSW - Jessica Page


Friday, 21st May 2010
Cem Turhan
My Student Wardrobe samples and highlights students' favourite and personal pieces in their wardrobes.

Jessica Page is a first year History Student from Hertford in Langwith College.

I brought the jacket this easter. It's a nice summer jacket and blazers are in this season. It was from New Look and was about £20.

I got the jeans in Joy from York, about a year ago before I even knew I was coming here. They were £7 in the sale.

My mum brought me the necklace from a Victorian market in the village of Hertford.

The jumper was my Grandfather's and it reminds me of him. It's nice to work in because it's snuggly.

The boots are from a shop in York called 'MoNO Shoes' and I got then in 2nd term. They were around £40.
