Join our team: how and where


The yorker massive
Monday, 7th January 2008
Daniel Ashby
Monday Week 2, CETLE (White Rose Centre, Vanbrugh). Time to be confirmed - check this space.

If you want to be part of something different then you've come to the right place. Here's five reasons to join our journalism team and come on Monday to find out more:

1) We're the only 24-hour news provider on campus, uncensored and independent.

2) Seven hundred people check the website everyday.

3) When you join our news team, you get trained in media law, news writing, TV camera work, editing and news presenting.

4) You can report the news, review restaurants, critique gigs and analyse events.

5) We're only at the tip of the iceberg. In 2008 we will be investing in an even better homepage and new projects.

The yorker is the biggest student limited company on campus, with over 120 members. We're the first student website to get nominated for a national award within 4 months of operation.

Get involved. Join the fray.
