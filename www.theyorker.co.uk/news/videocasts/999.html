Talking shop with Ramsey Street's Harold Bishop


Ian Smith plays Cinderella's father in the pantomime
Friday, 7th December 2007
Jo Shelley
Lucy Taylor
Neighbours veteran Ian Smith - a.k.a. Harold Bishop - has left Erinsborough behind this Christmas to appear in the York Opera House pantomime.

Not one to miss out on a celebrity interview, The Yorker took a quick break from its Christmas shopping to have a chat with "Jelly Belly", Ramsey Street’s oldest resident.

We ask:

> What does he think about the soap's new, racy image?

> What happens when he bumps into Kylie?

> And is it really always sunny in Ramsey Street?

This videocast was produced by Jo Shelley and Lucy Taylor.
