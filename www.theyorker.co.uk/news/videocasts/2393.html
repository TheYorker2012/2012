As we had a limited time-frame within which to film this videocast, we decided to focus on the central area of campus. between market square and Derwent, as this is where most students are during the day.

Students were picked at random and no one college was included or left out purposefully.

Hopefully the debate tomorrow will give graduate and undergraduate students a chance to express their views on the issue.
