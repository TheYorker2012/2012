Langwith Arts festival: exclusive video footage


Dancesoc
Friday, 5th June 2009
Anna Goldbeck-Wood
Fiona Lavelle
Recalling the first ever founding Langwith Arts festival, as created by Langwith Provost Dr John Issitt in 2006, the May bank holiday was this year once again alive with an eclectic programme, ranging from poetry, to crafts, to comedy, to opera.

One of the biggest features of the event was dance. Dance Soc performed from disciplines including salsa, jazz, street and tap, and we were on hand with a camera incase you missed out.
