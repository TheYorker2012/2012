Flash Mob organised for Ibiza Rave


Flash Mob
Friday, 12th June 2009
Fiona Lavelle
With the final Volume event of the year approaching, a surprise rave occurred today at 12.20 on Vanbrugh Paradise. 20 or so students burst out of Vanbrugh and danced Ibiza Rave style for a couple of minutes before dispersing.

Wearing lycra tights, bright legwarmers, UV colours and Converse, they recreated a night in Ziggy’s but in full daylight.

Despite selecting a usually busy moment of the day, not many students saw the wonderful display of colour and energy. Ravers, however, were undeterred, dancing enthusiastically.

It was an exciting preview into what promises to be one of the greatest Volume events of the year.

One raver, Tom McCarthy, commented: “I can’t believe we dance like that when we’re drunk. I’m knackered already. Come to Volume Week 8!”

Another student, Henri Ward, said: “Today was just a fantastic example of the kind of excitement that Ibiza Rave will provide not only for Vanbrugh students but for the entire student body.”
