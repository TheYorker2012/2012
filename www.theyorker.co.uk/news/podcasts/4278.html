A call for your art


Monday, 15th March 2010
Pete Burgess

Here at The Yorker Arts Section, change is afoot.

Often impressed by student creativity out there in the real world, we've wanted to offer an online platform for a wide variety of original art for some time. With the recent reshuffles on the Arts home page we now boast a box to show off your creative achievements.

For the initiative to work, we need to hear from imaginative souls – especially those of you who like to indulge in:

  • creative writing
  • photography
  • musical composition of any kind
  • film-making
  • any other form of art that will translate favourably onto our humble pages.

The Original Work box will go live at the start of next term. So, if you'd like to get involved, drop us an email at creativity@theyorker.co.uk.

We'd love to hear from you.
