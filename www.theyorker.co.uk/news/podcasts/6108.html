Photography: Christmas


Christmas
Wednesday, 15th December 2010
Izzi Dixon
Photo Soc is now running fortnightly photography competitions, with members taking a theme as inspiration for their pictures. As this fortnight's entry coincided with the end of term here in York, "Christmas" was both an appropriate and fun theme.

This week's winner is Oliver Woods.

About the photo: "Snow coming so early in York made the whole place so Christmassy. I got up especially at sunrise to get this and it really feels festive!"
