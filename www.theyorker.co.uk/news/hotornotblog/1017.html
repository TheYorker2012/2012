Slouchy hats: so hot right now
Monday, 10th December 2007
Dominic Freeston
From fabulous to frightening; frivolous praise and scorn for whatâ€™s Hot and whatâ€™s Not

Really Hot

  • Slouchy Hats

Big, chunky knitted hats are warm, flattering and undeniably unisex. Oversized berets and crocheted cloches are casual cool and can dress down an otherwise smart outfit. Whilst I’d advise boys to stay away from the glittery versions, everyone should embrace the slouchy beanie. A little trend whoring every now and again never did anyone any harm.

  • Referring to yourself in the third person on Facebook

The ideal way to boost your sense of self importance. Laura is hoping she gets lots of Christmas presents. Laura is wondering what she’ll have to eat for dinner. Laura is enjoying feeling regal every time she updates her status. Laura is wishing she could talk about herself in this fashion all the time.

  • The ice cream van

Incredibly, the ice cream van of Tang Hall (and presumably further afield) is still going strong, despite the fact that Christmas will be upon us in two weeks. Given that I’m gnawing on a frozen hazelnut yogurt as I type, this I think, is fantastic. The ice cream people have recognised that not everyone craves a warm mince pie every night in December; some of us enjoy a dessert to match the temperature outside. Of course, what’s really hot is the notion that whatever you want is available whenever you want it. Terrific. Veruca Salt anyone?

  • Having a good Christmas

So make sure you do.

Really Not

  • National Express taking over GNER

My ‘what’s not’ this week was going to be the new GNER website. I was on it the other day, and came away confused and frustrated, ticket-less and quite angry. I couldn’t believe that good old reliable GNER had made things so complicated. Everything was different, I didn’t know where to click or where to look, and – inconvenience of inconveniences – I had to re-register. Now today, fully prepared to pinpoint and explain my website disdain I typed www.gner.co.uk in my little internet window. I was met with a white screen, and a rush of pure horror as I read, “From 9th December 2007 National Express took over running the popular East Coast rail franchise - previously operated by GNER. You will now be redirected to the nationalexpresseastcoast.com website”. This is just AWFUL, I can’t believe it. I LOVE GNER! Yeah, yeah, they were having a few financial problems…I knew something like this would happen, but so soon? So National Express? I feel bad that I ever considered suggesting that anything to do with my beloved Great North Eastern Railway could possibly be not hot. Mostly though, I feel bad that the lovely, imposing, navy blue trains are no doubt going to be painted white and blue – not hot at all, and the website is still bad.

  • Over accessorising with one colour

This seems to happen a lot around Christmas, especially with the colour red. Why is it that people – usually even sensible people – who buy something red, suddenly turn into accessory monsters, convinced that taking ‘co-ordinated’ to a whole new level is a good idea? Red shoes? Great choice – fun, flirty, fabulous! Whip out some gorgeous red lipstick, and leave it at that. Just because your shoes are red, this doesn’t mean your bag, nail varnish, necklace, scarf, earrings, belt and bracelets should all be too. In fact, it means they shouldn’t.

Reunion: not hot

  • The Spice Girls

I know the tour has had rave reviews, and they all look better than they have in years, and Cavalli designed the costumes, but I’m just not feeling the reunion. Were they all (obviously with the exception of Posh) running out of money?
