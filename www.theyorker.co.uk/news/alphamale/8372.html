Only a few days left
Thursday, 19th January 2012
Laura Reynolds
Written by Faye Priestley

Guys! If you’re going out in the New Year, put away those Christmas jumpers, bad stocking fillers and clothes you wore and tore last year, and HIT THE SALES! The end of January marks your last opportunity to be as savvy as your female counterparts and pick up some designer pieces at discounted prices to last you throughout the year.

Top 5 Guy Buys:

  • If money is tight after Christmas no need to worry, pick up this Ralph Lauren polo for just over half price at £39.95 (Was: £75.00). Team with jeans or beige chinos for a night out, and with short sleeves, it will see you all the way through to your summer holidays!
  • Girls if you want to spoil him on Valentine’s Day, here’s the perfect gift. This crew neck cashmere jumper can be worn casually, to work or on a night out. At £259.00 it’s a massive half off, originally priced, £519.00. It’s a classic piece that won’t go out of fashion. Cashmere is always worth the money, just be careful how you wash it!
  • If it’s a new shirt you’re looking for, look no further. Role the sleeves up when you’re hot, down when you’re queuing and cold! Left open or buttoned up, this shirt is really versatile and allows for multiple looks without anyone noticing, it’s the same shirt! At £92.95 this Rag and Bone shirt is available at Harrods for less than half price.
  • This Theory cardigan started at £349.00 and is available to you for ¼ of the price at only £84.95. Buy in black and wear with anything! Perfect with graphic tees or layer with a scarf and a jacket. Wear open or buttoned for 2 different looks.
  • At less than half price, this D&G jumper is a steal at only £99.95(originally £199.00). Where under a jacket, over a collar or alone and stay bang on the ‘chunky knit’ trend we’ve seen this winter. With more cold weather still to come this D&G piece is an investment worth making.

All Available until Sunday 22nd Jan at www.harrods.com
