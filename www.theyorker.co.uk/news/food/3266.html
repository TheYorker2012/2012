Strawberry cheesecake


Tuesday, 16th June 2009
Stacey Go
Cheesecake isn't very expensive or difficult to make, since two of its main ingredients, cream cheese and Rich Tea biscuits, seem to be staples of typical student cuisine. With the sunny weather we've been having, making cheesecake is perfect for an outdoor picnic this summer.

Ingredients:

  • 300g cream cheese
  • 8 rich tea biscuits
  • Strawberries for decoration
  • 50g butter
  • 150ml double cream

Directions:

1. Crush the biscuits until only crumbs remain. Melt the butter and coat the crumbs in the mixture. Pour into a cake tin to form a biscuit base.

2. Pour the double cream and cream cheese into a mixing bowl, then proceed to beat the mixture until thick and smooth.

3. Slowly spoon the mixture into the cake tin, then leave to cool in the fridge overnight. By the next morning, you should have a delicious and tasty cheesecake. Sprinkle with a handful of strawberries and enjoy!
