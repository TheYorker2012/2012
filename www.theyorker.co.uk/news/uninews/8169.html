YUSU & GSA results announced


(Photo source: YUSU)
Monday, 12th December 2011
Jason Rose
The results of both YUSU and York GSA’s autumn student elections have been published, and whilst some positions were highly contested, many were left empty.

In YUSU’s elections, filling many of its committee positions and three NUS delegate openings, students were elected into 25 roles while 22 were left unfilled.

The GSA had a similar ratio, with 12 filled and 10 unfilled roles, despite having a massive amount of competition for the position of Welfare Officer, a sabbatical post.

Though the November elections are notorious for leaving vacancies, 47% and 45% vacancies are more than either organisation will want to have seen.

The results of the elections are as follows:

YUSU

  • NUS National Conference Delegates: Ben Dilks, Nell Beecham and Zahra Latif
  • Campaigns Committee - Ordinary Members: Chrissie Baker, Jamie Fisher and Peter Hollingsworth
  • Democracy Committee - Ordinary Members: Curtis Sinclair, Joel Hellewell, Josh Allen and Nicholas Hall
  • Disabled Students' Committee - LGBT Rep: Alix Penn
  • Entertainments Committee - Ents Assistant: Alexander Clackson
  • Mature Students' Committee - Secretary: Chris Barnes
  • Racial Equality Committee - Press & Publicity Rep: Beatrice Tesconi
  • Racial Equality Committee - Ordinary Members: Fathma Khalid, Hussein Kesvani, Raveen Sagar
  • RAG Committee - Woodstock Coordinator: Emily Hogan
  • Women's Committee - Secretary: Ophelia Groth
  • Women's Committee - Press & Publicity: Gillian Love and Sophie Miller
  • Women's Committee - Lesbian & Bisexual Women's Rep: Saskia Papadakis
  • Women's Committee - International Women's Rep: Jenny Michel
  • Women's Committee - Ordinary Members: Charlotte Rowland and Josie Field

Not filled: Five Ents’ Assistant positions, two Women’s Committee ordinary positions, eight Disabled Committee reps, Environment and Ethics ordinary member, Mature Students’ Committee Social Secretary, Racial Equality Committee’s Secretary position and four Women’s Committee positions.

Unfilled positions will be filled at Open Meetings of the Network.

The LGBT Committee was elected at a meeting on Wednesday of week nine and voting for LGBT Officer is open until Monday of week ten (December 12).

GSA

  • Welfare Officer: Keke Zu
  • Senate Representative: Dave Johnson
  • Chair of Council: Andrea Daniels
  • Council Representatives: Kirstie Jones
  • Events Officers: Elena Gorianova and Bai Yi Zhuo
  • Sports Officer: Ali Bostancioglu and Xue Han
  • Off-campus Representative: Andrew Marriot
  • PGWT Representative: Ajith Jinjil
  • LGBT Representative: Ken Leach
  • International Students’ Representative: Tong Che

Not filled: PGCE Representative, Part-Time Students Representative, Distance Learning Student’s Representative, Communication Officer, Communities, Charities and Volunteering Officer, a Council representative, Campaigns Officer, Disabilities’ Representative, Gender Equality Representative and Students With Dependents’ Representative
