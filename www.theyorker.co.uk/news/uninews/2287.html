Week in Pictures: Grease


Monday, 17th November 2008
Antonia Moura
The Yorker brings you pictures of this week's Grease performance.

This week saw a performance of Grease in Central Hall courtesy of Happily Ever After Soc.


The cast and crew

The T-Birds were looking good in their leather jackets and hair gel.

The Pink Ladies sported equally amazing costumes and gave a great performance in them.

Daniel Sofaer was electrifying as Kenickie

Nicola Carter gave her all as the feisty Rizzo.

Sophie Louise Brown as Sandy and Sam McCormick as Danny brought some summer love to York's autumn days.
