'Campus's most hated man' assaulted in Tang Hall


Student assaulted in Tang Hall
Thursday, 13th March 2008
Daniel Ashby
The student journalist who calls himself "campus's most hated man" has been violently assaulted in Tang Hall.

Adam Thorn and two friends were attacked while walking back from the Black Bull quiz at 12:30am.

He said: "We stepped onto the road politely to let the man walk past on the pavement and he just turned and started hitting me in the face. There seemed no reason for it whatsoever.

"He smashed me several times in the face before knocking Harry's glasses across the road and giving him a nose bleed.

"It's a scary world out there."

Thorn said that he and his friends were simply walking down the road chatting and had not provoked the attack at all.

"I just stood there completely bemused while he whacked me in the face."

The third friend, who is the author of Vision's notorious "Campus Playboy" column, got away without injury.

The man is described as being of medium build and having short, spiky brown hair.

The three students spent the next day in the police station testifying.

The police have a good idea who the attacker is and are hoping to take him to court.
