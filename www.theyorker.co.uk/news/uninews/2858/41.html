YUSU Election Results Night 2009

Saturday, 14th March 2009
Amy Benziane
Marie Thouaille
The Yorker reported live from The Courtyard, bringing you the latest news as it came in. We voiced your views, we spoke to candidates and current officers and had fun throughout the night. We hope you enjoyed it too!

Additional reporting from Chris Travis, Antonia Moura, Rachel Basch, Chloe Dean, Claire Curtis and Laura Lennkh.

01:51 According to Tom Scott our elections have seen the highest turnout of all the universities in the country. Although 23% isn't exactly impressive.
